Quick answers

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. On your iPhone, open Settings → your name → Subscriptions, choose Atelier Daily, and change or cancel there. Cancelling keeps your Couture membership active until the end of the period you've paid for.

I reinstalled the app — where is my membership?

Open Profile in the app and tap Restore Purchases. Your Couture membership is tied to your Apple Account, so it follows you to any new device or reinstall.

The weather or my city looks wrong

Check that location access is on in Settings → Atelier Daily → Location. If you'd rather not share your location, that's fine — the app styles you for a default city instead, and you can set your city in your Profile.

My daily reminder stopped arriving

Open Profile → Daily ritual, switch the reminder off and back on, and use Send a test to confirm. Also check that notifications are allowed in Settings → Atelier Daily → Notifications.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app, in your Profile. Prefer we do it? Email atelierdailyapp@gmail.com from the address you signed up with and we'll take care of it.

A shopping link earned you a commission?

Sometimes, yes. When the stylist suggests a piece to complete a look, the outbound retail link may earn us a small commission at no cost to you. It never influences what the stylist selects — the styling comes first, always.
