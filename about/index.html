About

The styling is the luxury,
not the shopping.

Atelier Daily · California

Atelier Daily began with a simple observation: most of us own a good wardrobe and still stand in front of it every morning with nothing to wear. The problem was never the closet. It was that no one was styling it.

So we built a stylist. Atelier Daily photographs the pieces you already own and composes a complete look every morning — head to toe, shoes and bag included — styled to your city's real forecast. It builds editorial moodboards from your own clothes, shows looks on you, packs your trips day by day, and when a look genuinely calls for something you don't own, it finds that one piece. Never a feed. Never fast fashion for its own sake.

Independent, founder-built

Atelier Daily is an independent app, designed and built by its founder, Cristina Rosas, in California — not a corporate product with a styling feature bolted on. Every design decision follows one rule: it has to feel luxurious for everyone, whether you're on the free Atelier plan or the Couture membership. The craft is never the paid tier.

What we believe

  • Dress for the day you'll actually have — the forecast is the most honest stylist there is.
  • Rewearing is elegance — the best-dressed people repeat themselves beautifully.
  • Style is for everyone — women's, men's, and everything open in between, with the same editorial eye.
  • Buy less, choose better — when we recommend a piece, it's one considered piece, disclosed honestly. Some outbound retail links may earn us a small commission at no cost to you; it never influences what the stylist selects.
